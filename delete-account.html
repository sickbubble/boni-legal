Account Deletion

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Boni account and what happens to your data. Boni is developed by the Boni team ("we," "our," or "us").

Delete your account in the app

You can delete your account directly from within Boni at any time:

  1. Open the Boni app and sign in.
  2. Go to More > Settings > Profile (the Edit Profile screen).
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm, then verify your identity when prompted (enter your password, or re-authenticate with Google or Apple — this is required for your security).
  5. Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

What gets deleted

  • Your account, profile, and sign-in identity are permanently removed.
  • On iOS, your Sign in with Apple authorization for Boni is revoked.
  • You are removed from any families you share with other members.
  • Any active subscription entitlement is disconnected from the app. Note: deleting your account does not cancel a paid subscription — manage or cancel subscriptions in your Google Play or Apple App Store account settings.

Shared family data

If your pets, logs, or photos live in a family shared with other members, that content remains available to those members after you leave, so the family isn't left without its records. If you want all pet data and uploaded media associated with you to be erased completely, request it by email (below) and we will remove it.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, or you want complete erasure of all associated pet data and media, email us and we will process your request:

Email: boni.app.contact@gmail.com
Please send from the email address associated with your account, or include the email/sign-in method you used, so we can verify and locate your account. We aim to respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Once processed, your personal data is deleted. Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics and service-improvement purposes. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
